Clinical

The Clinical category of HL7® FHIR® resources includes Summary, Diagnostics, Medications, Care Provision, Request and Response.

Summary

Clinical resources are used throughout the patient record to record summaries of status and health history. This includes recording information around allergies, conditions, and procedures, among others.

Implemented Resources

  • AllergyIntolerance
  • Condition
  • Procedure

Diagnostics

The Diagnostic resources are concerned with the process of observing, testing, and diagnosing a patient. Note that, once a diagnosis is confirmed, it is recorded as a Condition so it becomes part of the historical patient record.

The DiagnosticReport resource typically provides a set of textual information and interpretation after performing a diagnostic service, such as a Radiology report or Pathology report.

The Observation resource primarily records discrete values, answers, and results, such as clinical laboratory data and vital signs.

Select here to see the detailed categorization and full set of resources that fall into this category in the HL7® FHIR® Standard.

Implemented Resources

Medications

Resources in the Medications category are used to support the immunization and medication processes.

Select https://hl7.org/fhir/r4/medications-module.html for detailed categorization and full set of resources that fall into this category in the HL7® FHIR® Standard.

  • MedicationRequest
  • Immunization
