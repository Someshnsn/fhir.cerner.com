Entities

Entities are used to track, relate, and locate things and people across the healthcare spectrum. They include things like Location, but also the Person resource. The Person resource is used to index a real world person providing a means to identify the same person across different healthcare organizations and roles.

Click here to see the detailed categorization and full set of resources that fall into this category in the HL7® FHIR® Standard.
